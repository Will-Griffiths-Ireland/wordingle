How to play

Drag a word onto the first tile where you think it starts.

Wordingle is a clue-based word search. Each round gives you a prompt, a list of possible words, and a letter grid. Some listed words match the prompt and are hidden in the grid. Some are decoys.

Goal

  • Find every word that matches the clue.
  • Correct words are placed in the grid. Decoy words are not.
  • Found words stay visible in the word list, greyed out with a line through them.

Controls

  • Desktop: drag a word onto the tile where it starts. Drag slightly across the tile to choose the direction.
  • Mobile: tap a word to select it, then tap-and-swipe from the starting tile in the word direction.
  • Words can run horizontally, vertically, diagonally down, or diagonally up.

Scoring

  • Correct words are worth 1,000 base points.
  • Words found before the first reshuffle score ×3.
  • Words found after one reshuffle score ×2.
  • After that, words score the normal 1,000 points.
  • Wrong placements or decoy words reduce your score by 100.

Reshuffle mode

  • Challenge rounds reshuffle every 30 seconds.
  • Already found words remain locked safely in place.
  • The global colour theme changes at each reshuffle and remains until the next one.

Progression

  • Challenge mode has child and adult paths with 10 stages each.
  • Later stages use more abstract, linguistic, and cryptic clues.
  • Free play unlocks after completing a challenge path.
