How to play

Drag a word onto the first tile where you think it starts.

Goal: find every hidden word in the letter grid.

How to answer: on desktop, drag a word onto the first letter of its path. On mobile, tap a word to select it, then tap-and-swipe from the starting tile in the word direction. The swipe can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal.

Progress: found words stay visible in the list, but turn grey with a line through them so you can track what remains without the layout jumping around.

Challenge mode: progress through fixed levels. Child challenges scale from simple 3-letter words and tiny grids up to teen-level puzzles. Adult challenges scale through longer words, tougher grids and faster reshuffles.

Reshuffle: unlocked tiles periodically flip away, vanish, return and reveal a newly arranged grid. Found words stay locked.

Free play: unlocks after completing either the child or adult challenge path. Scores are stored separately from challenge scores.
